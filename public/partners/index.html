The 15-minute flow

01
Share your Facebook Page

In Business Suite → Settings → Pages, share your Page asset with Artist Influence - MAIN. This is what lets the ads run as you.

02
Share your Instagram

If IG is connected to your Page, sharing the Page covers it. Otherwise: Business Suite → Settings → Instagram accounts → Share with Artist Influence - MAIN.

03
Share your Pixel (only if you have one)

Events Manager → Settings → Share dataset → enter our Business ID. Skip if you don't have one, we'll set up tracking on our side.

04
Send fan lists

SMS / email exports from Laylo, ManyChat, Hypeddit, Shopify, anything. CSV, screenshot, whatever, we'll turn them into custom audiences and lookalikes.

05
Confirm and launch

Email Reza, we run a 10-minute QA pass, then go live the same day in most cases.

06
Add Reza as Admin (powers your dashboard)

Business Settings → People → Add reza@artistinfluence.com as Admin. This unlocks live reporting, so we can hand you a dashboard that refreshes every 24 hours.
